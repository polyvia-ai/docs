Polyvia empowers Developers and Teams with visual search and reasoning infrastructure. Extract, index, and reason over charts, slides, and diagrams — turning scattered visuals into a queryable index of facts.

## Polyvia Engine

Transform visual data into structured, connected knowledge with our three-stage pipeline.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Visual Extraction" icon="eye">
    VLM-OCR extracts visual logic from charts, tables, diagrams, and infographics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Visual Indexing" icon="diagram-project">
    Facts-Source Ontology disambiguates facts and connects them into a single source of truth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agentic Reasoning" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Search and query with visual citations — answers grounded in source documents.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
