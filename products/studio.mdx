Polyvia Studio is a web application for knowledge-work teams to search and explore visual data. Find answers hidden in millions of charts, slides, and reports.

## Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visual Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Query across your entire document collection with natural language
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audit-Ready Citations" icon="quote-right">
    Every answer traced to source document, page, and visual element
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-Document Reasoning" icon="diagram-project">
    Connect facts across 10,000+ documents automatically
  </Card>
  <Card title="Easy Upload" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    Drag and drop PDFs, slides, and reports to build your knowledge base
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
