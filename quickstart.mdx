## Get started in three steps

Follow these steps to integrate Polyvia into your workflow and start extracting insights from your visual documents.

### Step 1: Access Polyvia

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="right-to-bracket" title="Log in to Polyvia Studio">
    Navigate to [Polyvia Studio](https://app.polyvia.ai) and sign up or log in to your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Obtain your API Key">
    Go to the **Settings** section in Polyvia Studio to generate your API key. This key will be used for all API requests and MCP integrations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Ingest Your Documents

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="file-import" title="Upload via Polyvia Studio">
    Drag and drop your PDFs, slides, or other visual documents into the upload zone. Polyvia will extract visual logic and index facts automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="code" title="Upload via API">
    Use our `/ingest` endpoint to programmatically upload documents. Check the [API Reference](/api-reference/introduction) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Query Your Visual Knowledge Base

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="browser" title="Search via Polyvia Studio">
    Open a chat session in Polyvia Studio and ask questions. Get answers with audit-ready citations pointing to exact documents, pages, and visual sources.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="code" title="Search via API">
    Use the `/query` endpoint to programmatically search your knowledge base. See the [API Reference](/api-reference/endpoint/query) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
