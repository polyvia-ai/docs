Polyvia AI empowers Developers and Teams with agentic knowledge retrieval. Chat with documents, dismantle data silos, and turn unstructured data into actionable decisions.

## Our Pipeline

Transform unstructured chaos into structured knowledge with our multimodal pipeline.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ingest" icon="file-arrow-up">
    Upload PDF, Docx, PPT, and Audio/Video files securely.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Parse & Extract" icon="microchip">
    Documents converted to Markdown/JSON. Charts parsed for data points.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Structure" icon="database">
    Data is embedded into a Vector Database and linked via Knowledge Graph.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agentic Retrieval" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Agents traverse the graph to answer complex queries with citations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
