## Get started in three steps

Follow these steps to integrate Polyvia AI into your workflow and start extracting actionable intelligence from your documents.

### Step 1: Access the Dashboard

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="right-to-bracket" title="Log in to Polyvia AI">
    Navigate to the [Polyvia AI Dashboard](https://app.polyvia.ai) and sign up or log in to your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Obtain your API Key">
    Go to the **Settings** section in your dashboard to generate your API key. This key will be used for all API requests and AI tool integrations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Ingest Your First Document

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="file-import" title="Upload via Dashboard">
    Drag and drop your PDF, Docx, or media files into the upload zone. Our pipeline will automatically begin parsing and structuring the data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="code" title="Upload via API">
    Use our REST API to programmatically upload documents for processing. Check the [API Reference](/api-reference/introduction) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Query Your Agent

<Accordion icon="comment-dots" title="Chat with your documents">
  Open a new chat session and start asking questions. Our agents will traverse your knowledge graph to provide accurate answers with direct citations from your uploaded documents.
</Accordion>
